Frequently asked

Is there a fee to apply?

No. There is no application fee.

Can I bring a partner or family?

The apartment is set up for a single resident. Partners or family members may be able to visit for part of the stay — please discuss when you apply.

Is Italian required?

No, though some basic Italian will enrich your daily life in Marradi. English is understood in most practical situations.

When are the residency seasons?

Spring: April through June. Autumn: September through November. We host one artist per season.
