Tuscany, Italy

A place apart for artists who need time

The Marradi Residency offers uninterrupted time and space to work in the Apennine mountains between Florence and Ravenna.

Artists of all disciplines are invited to spend weeks or months in one of Tuscany's most quietly beautiful towns — painting, writing, composing, or developing work free from the pressures of everyday life.
