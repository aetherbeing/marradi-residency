Give to Marradi

The Marradi Residency is sustained entirely by the generosity of individual donors who believe that artists need time, space, and the experience of place.

Make a Gift ↗

Why We Need Support

The residency operates as a nonprofit program. Your gift covers housing costs, the living stipend provided to each resident, the maintenance and preparation of the apartment and studio, and the modest administrative costs of running the program.

Without donor support, none of this is possible. Every gift — at any level — directly enables an artist to spend weeks in Marradi doing the work they need to do.

Levels of Support

All donors receive our seasonal newsletter with updates from current and former residents, reflections on the program, and photographs from Marradi.

$100 Friend
  • Seasonal newsletter
  • Named acknowledgment on our website
  • Our sincere gratitude
$500 Supporter
  • Everything at Friend level
  • A handwritten note from the current resident
  • Invitation to our annual gathering
$2,500 Patron
  • Everything at Supporter level
  • A work on paper from a resident artist
  • Named in residency program materials
  • Private conversation with the director
$10,000 Benefactor
  • Everything at Patron level
  • Named residency season in your honor
  • Original artwork from a resident's stay
  • Invitation to visit Marradi during a residency
  • Recognition in all program materials

Other Ways to Give

Recurring gifts
Monthly or annual recurring gifts are especially valuable — they allow us to plan ahead and commit to artists earlier in the year. Any amount helps.

In-kind support
We welcome offers of materials, equipment, or services that support the residency or its residents. Please contact us to discuss.

Estate gifts
If you are considering including the Marradi Residency in your estate plans, we would be honored to speak with you about how such a gift might be structured.

Tax Information

The Marradi Residency is a fiscally sponsored nonprofit project. Contributions may be tax-deductible to the extent permitted by law. Please consult your tax advisor.

Donors at all levels receive written acknowledgment of their gift for tax purposes within two weeks of receiving your donation.

For questions about giving, please contact us directly.
