Apply

2026 — Invitation Only. For our inaugural year, we are inviting a select group of writers to join us in Marradi. Open applications will begin in Fall 2026 for the 2027 season.

If you're interested in being considered for a future session, please contact us.

2027 Onward

Eligibility

We welcome applications from:

  • Fiction writers
  • Poets
  • Creative nonfiction writers
  • Literary translators (especially Italian–English)

Requirement: at least one published work (book, chapbook, or literary magazine publication).

How to Apply

Application requirements

  • BioAuthor's biography
  • CVFull CV including list of publications
  • MotivationLetter of motivation — 1 paragraph
  • ProjectWhat you will be working on in Marradi
  • SessionsWhich session(s) you would like to attend
  • SampleWriting sample — 5 pages, double-spaced
  • Fee€30 application fee (non-refundable, via Submittable)

Joint applicants: indicate the name of your co-applicant in your application. Your co-applicant must also apply independently and list your name.

Resident Commitments

What we ask of residents
