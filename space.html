The Apartment

The Space

The residency apartment is located at Via Fabroni 6, on the third floor of a historic building in central Marradi, a five-minute walk from the train station.

Note: there is no elevator.

The apartment has three bedrooms, a shared kitchen and dining area, one bathroom, and a small loggia. Two writers share the space during each session, with private quarters on opposite sides of the apartment and shared common areas in between.

Additional Workspace

Beyond the apartment

Writers can also work at the Centro Studi Campaniani, the town library (open 3 days per week and Saturday mornings), or local cafes. Marradi has everything you need within walking distance: cafes, restaurants, bakeries, a supermarket, pharmacy, and shops — all in the village center.
