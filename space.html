The Space

A restored apartment in the heart of Marradi

A private, fully equipped apartment steps from the town center — comfortable for living, configured for working.

Living area
Studio

The Apartment

The residency apartment occupies the upper floor of a restored stone building on one of Marradi's central streets. High ceilings, wide-plank floors, and south-facing windows bring light throughout the day.

The apartment includes a bedroom, a well-equipped kitchen, a living room, and a bathroom. Everything needed for daily life is in place — linens, cookware, a washing machine, WiFi, and a small library of books about the region.

The building has two entrances and a private stairwell. Artists have the floor to themselves during their stay.

Apartment interior
Studio space

The Studio

The studio is a large, flexible room with north light and direct access from the apartment. It is configured to the needs of each resident in advance of their arrival — we work with artists to understand what they need.

Standard equipment includes a large work table, easel, storage shelving, a sink, and movable track lighting. The floor and walls are easily protected for messy work.

Writers and musicians are equally welcome — the studio works just as well as a dedicated writing room or practice space, away from the domestic areas of the apartment.

What's Provided

  • Bedroom Queen bed with quality linens, wardrobe, bedside tables and reading lamps.
  • Kitchen Fully equipped with cookware, espresso maker, refrigerator, oven, and pantry staples to get you started.
  • Studio Large work room with north light, configurable to the artist's discipline and practice.
  • WiFi High-speed internet throughout the apartment and studio.
  • Location Two-minute walk to the town square, weekly market, train station, and local restaurants.
  • Outdoor access A small terrace off the living room, and the Apennine trails begin minutes from the front door.
